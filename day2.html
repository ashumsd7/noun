Sentence
Having Complete meaning (Group of words). Arrnagement of words.
Structure of a sentence:

SUBJECT + VERB + OBJECT

Types of Sentences
  • ASSERTIVE SENTENCE:
    • Affirmative (Positive)
    • S + HV + MV + O
      Radha has written a letter.
    • Negative Sentence
    • S + HV +NOT MV + O
      Radha has not written a letter.
  • Interrogative Senetence
    • Close ended interrogative Sentences
    • HV + S + MV + O+ ?

      • Do you know Rahul?
      You answer will be Yes/No
    • Open Eneded interrogative Sentences
    • Question Word + HV + S + MV + O + ?

      • What do you know about Rahul?
      Your answer will not be Yes/No, here you you will answer in a sentence.
  • Imperative Sentence: (Request/Order/Command)
    • V1st Form + Obj
    • Shut the door.
    • Do + Not + Object
    • Do not close the door
